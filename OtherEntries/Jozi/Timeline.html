Timeline of Johannesburg

  1. 1886 - Johannesburg established after gold found in vicinity.
  2. 1887 - St. Mary's Church built. Johannesburg Stock Exchange(JSE) founded.
  3. 1890 - Jeppe High School for Boys was founded.
  4. 1891 - Prison was built.
  5. 1895 - Railway operational.
  6. 1865 - Uprising against Boer government. Braamfontein explosion.
  7. 1897 - Park Station opens.
  8. 1898 - St. John's College founded.
  9. 1900 - Town captured by the British during Second Boer War.
  10. 1903 - JSE building constructed.
  11. 1904 - Joburg Zoo established.
